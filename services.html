Cataloging
From $149 per ASIN

Cataloging covers all listing-related assistance and content services for ASIN creation and optimization. Every product's discoverability on the marketplace begins with how its listing is structured, and most underperforming listings suffer from weak titles, poorly researched keywords, and bullet points that fail to convert.

Our cataloging service starts with in-depth keyword research using search volume and relevancy data to identify the exact terms buyers use to find products like yours. We craft search-optimized titles within character limits that balance keyword placement with readability. Bullet points are written to highlight the most important product features and benefits in a scannable format that supports purchase decisions.

We also handle backend search term optimization, ensuring all available keyword space is utilized without duplication. For new ASINs, we manage the full creation workflow including flat file builds, variation setup, and category-specific attribute completion. All content is written to comply with marketplace guidelines to avoid suppression or policy flags.

Advertising Optimization
From $199 per month

Advertising Optimization covers full management of your sponsored advertisement campaigns to maximize return on ad spend while minimizing wasted budget. Running campaigns without a structured optimization process means paying for impressions and clicks that do not convert, while high-value keywords go underfunded.

Our service includes keyword research to identify high-intent search terms across broad, phrase, and exact match types. Campaigns are structured for maximum efficiency, separating auto and manual campaigns and isolating top performers to control bids precisely. Bids are reviewed and adjusted regularly based on performance data pulled directly through AdNexora.

Negative keyword management is applied systematically to eliminate irrelevant spend. We also manage Sponsored Brands, Sponsored Display, and DSP campaigns, including retargeting strategies to recapture browsers who viewed your listings. Dayparting rules are applied based on time-of-day performance trends identified through AdNexora, and campaign performance is reviewed with you on a regular cadence.

A+ Cataloging
From $249 per ASIN

A+ Cataloging, also known as Enhanced Brand Content, enables brand-registered sellers to replace the standard product description with a rich, visually structured experience on the product detail page. Well-executed A+ Content has a measurable impact on conversion rate, reduces return rates by setting accurate product expectations, and strengthens brand recognition.

Our A+ Cataloging service covers strategy, design, and content creation for standard and Premium A+ modules. We start by understanding your brand positioning and the key product benefits that most influence purchase decisions. From there, we design module layouts that guide the buyer through a clear, compelling visual story combining high-quality imagery, feature callouts, comparison charts, and brand storytelling elements.

For eligible brands, we also create Brand Stories, which appear across your full storefront and establish brand identity at the catalog level. All A+ Content is built to comply with current guidelines and is optimized for both desktop and mobile rendering. We handle the full submission process and monitor approval status through to publication.
