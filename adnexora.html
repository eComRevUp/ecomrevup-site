Data Use and Privacy

How We Use Your
Selling Account Data

eCom RevUp accesses your selling account data exclusively through authorized integrations to provide the reporting, analytics, and growth services you have engaged us for. Your data is yours. We act only as a service provider operating on your behalf.

Data Accessed
We access orders, advertising performance, FBA inventory, financial transactions, catalog items, and product pricing data solely to deliver the services you have authorized.
Purpose-Limited Use
Your data is used exclusively to provide reporting dashboards, advertising management, listing optimization, and related growth services. It is never used for any other purpose.
No Third-Party Sharing
Your selling account data is never sold, licensed, or shared with any third party. Access is restricted to authorized personnel delivering your contracted services.
Revocable at Any Time
You retain full control of your data. You may revoke our access to your selling account at any time, and we will promptly cease all data access and delete associated records upon request.
